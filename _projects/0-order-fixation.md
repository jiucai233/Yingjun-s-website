title: "Order Fixation in Skill-Based RL: Why Learned Skills Refuse to Recombine"
description: "First-author working paper. A causal-ablation study on SkiMo across Franka Kitchen and CALVIN, localizing the failure of zero-shot skill recombination to a self-sealing training loop rather than the weights: the frozen skill prior starves the replay buffer of counter-order successes and stale reward annotations dominate the value target, yet the same checkpoint learns the forbidden order from a fresh or relabeled buffer. Validated across four agents. In preparation for conference submission."
